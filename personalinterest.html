♥ PERSONAL INTEREST ♥

- MUSIC -

Music has always been a passion of mine. It serves as a conduit for expressing emotions that are sometimes difficult to articulate. The soothing and calming nature of music provides a form of solace. I have a particular fondness for classical music, with its intricate compositions and timeless melodies. However, my musical interests are not confined to one genre. I also enjoy the pulsating rhythms of electronic music, the catchy tunes of pop, and the powerful beats of rock.

- SCULPTING -

Sculpting, the art of moulding clay and other materials, brings me immense joy. There's something incredibly satisfying about crafting small, adorable objects with my own hands. Seeing the final product of my sculpting efforts is always a delightful experience. On occasion, I love to share this joy with others by gifting them my creations. The act of creating something tangible from a simple lump of clay is not just fun, but also deeply comforting.

- FLOWER ARRANGEMENT -

The art of arranging flowers is a creative and rewarding process. Each flower, with its unique beauty, contributes to a harmonious composition. Working with a variety of flowers allows me to explore their complementary nature. The final arrangement is not just visually pleasing, but also a reflection of the language of flowers. Occasionally, I share this joy by gifting my arrangements, spreading the beauty of nature and creativity.

- PHOTOGRAPHY -

Photography is an art form that I deeply appreciate. It allows me to capture moments in time, preserving them for future reflection. The ability to express a story, an emotion, or a perspective through a single image is powerful and inspiring. Whether it's a breathtaking landscape, a candid moment, or a carefully arranged still life, each photograph represents a unique perspective of the world.
