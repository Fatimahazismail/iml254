♥ IMPORTANT MOMENTS ♥

Field Trip

One of my favorite pictures was taken by my best friend, Amira, during our field trip to Cameron Highlands in 2019, after our PT3 examination. The picture, captured at a tea plantation, shows me looking out over the field. As someone who is shy and dislikes having my whole face captured in pictures, this one became my favorite. It encapsulates a moment of tranquility amidst the vast tea plantation. I remember spending quality time with Amira, taking pictures, chatting, and enjoying each other's company.

My Family Vacation

One of my most cherished memories is a family vacation we took in 2012. We embarked on a journey to Singapore, traveling by bus and immersing ourselves in the city for three days and two nights. This journey was not just with my immediate family, but also with my cousins, my aunt and uncle, my grandmother's younger sister, and my parents' close friends. We explored numerous intriguing places around Singapore and had the privilege of witnessing the New Year's fireworks show from our hotel room. The moments spent there, with my family and everyone else, are ones I hold dear.

High School Memories

A photograph from my final year of high school in 2021 brings back fond memories. As the chief school librarian, I was joined by Alia and Lee Pei Yee, who served as my assistants. Our shared experiences may not be numerous, but the joy of their company was always present. We shared many things, including our personalities. The moments spent with them are unforgettable.

Friendship

A picture from 2019 encapsulates the essence of my friend group, marking two years of our friendship. The group consisted of Khayrin, Asyikin, Husna, Tasya, myself, and Putri. Together, we embarked on numerous adventures, including a trip to Cameron Highlands. They taught me the art of living freely and not worrying excessively about the future. From gossiping and problem-solving to helping each other, sleepovers, and studying together, we did it all. I cherish those moments and miss them dearly.

Last Meeting

A video from 2021 captures the last day of our meeting. We received our SPM results that day, marking the last time the six of us were together. It was a bittersweet moment, knowing it would be a long time before we could meet again. Now, we are all busy studying in our respective fields at universities across Malaysia. I hope that one day, we will all sit together again, reminiscing about the times we missed each other, and celebrating our successes.
